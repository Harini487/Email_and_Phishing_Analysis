Description
======================================
A fraudulent email impersonating Coinbase, falsely alerting the recipient of suspicious account activity and an unauthorized phone number change (Case# 83899065). 

It presents fabricated login details (IPv6 address, Netherlands location, Chrome/Windows browser) to create urgency and tricks the victim into clicking a 'Secure My Account' button that redirects to an attacker-controlled phishing domain.


Artifact Analysis
======================================
Sender Analysis:
The sender address "noreply-coinbasewalletverifiying.irs.mantab@shangriladogchew.com" is clearly fraudulent — legitimate Coinbase emails originate exclusively from @coinbase.com domains. 

The address contains multiple red flags: misspelling of 'verifying', injection of 'irs' to imply authority, and use of a completely unrelated domain 'shangriladogchew.com'. 


URL Analysis:
The 'Secure My Account' button links to "https://amedyna.com/", confirmed malicious by VirusTotal. 

The low detection rate suggests the domain is newly registered or recently weaponized, a common tactic to evade blocklists. 

The domain has no affiliation with Coinbase. The hoverable URL visible in the email footer (https://amedyna.com/) further confirms the button destination is attacker-controlled and designed to harvest Coinbase credentials


Attachment Analysis:
None


Verdict
======================================
The email exhibits multiple critical indicators: spoofed sender domain impersonating Coinbase, fabricated suspicious activity alert with fake IP/location data, social engineering urgency via phone number change notification, and a 'Secure My Account' CTA redirecting to a VirusTotal-confirmed phishing domain (amedyna.com, flagged by Kaspersky), indicating that email is a clear phishing attemp.

As a result of the analysis, this email has been determined to be malicious.


Defense Actions
======================================
After performing a message trace, no other users within the organization received an email from this sender or with this subject line.

To prevent the malicious sender from sending any other email to the organization, I have blocked the "noreply-coinbasewalletverifiying.irs.mantab@shangriladogchew.com" email address on the email gateway.

Due to the malicious nature of the domain, I have blocked any incoming emails that contain "shangriladogchew.com" on the email gateway.

To ensure users are unable to access this malicious URL or domain, I have blocked "amedyna.com" on the EDR and on the Web Proxy.

Report the phishing URL to Kaspersky and submit for additional vendor review on VirusTotal to increase detection coverage.

Notified affected users to not click the link and to verify account status directly at coinbase.com

The email has been reported to Coinbase's abuse team at security@coinbase.com.
