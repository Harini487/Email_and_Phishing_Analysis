Verdict
======================================
This is an authentic, legitimate/genuine email Dropbox-generated password reset notification. 

All sender, URL, and infrastructure indicators confirm the email originated from Dropbox's official systems. 

The reset link leads only to the legitimate Dropbox platform, no credential harvesting risk exists if the link is not clicked.


Defense Actions
======================================
If the reset was not requested by the recipient, ignore and delete the email as instructed by Dropbox's own message. 

The recipient should log in directly to dropbox.com to verify account integrity and confirm no unauthorized access has occurred. 

As a precautionary measure, enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on the Dropbox account is strongly recommended. 

No gateway blocking or URL blacklisting is warranted as all infrastructure is legitimate. 

If unsolicited resets recur, consider reporting the pattern to Dropbox via abuse@dropbox.com.
